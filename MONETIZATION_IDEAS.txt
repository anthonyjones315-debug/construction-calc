1. Amazon Associates Affiliate Ads (proconstruct-20)
   - Live on sign-in page + home page secondary column
   - Targets contractor gear: laser measures, speed squares, tool belts, calc tools
   - Earns 1–8% commission per qualifying Amazon purchase
   - Opportunity: Add affiliate panels inside calculator result pages too — users
     are in "buy mode" right after running a concrete or framing estimate

2. Google AdSense
   - Infrastructure is in place (env var: NEXT_PUBLIC_ADSENSE_ID)
   - Currently requires user cookie consent before loading
   - Opportunity: Turn on Auto Ads for public pages — FAQ, Field Notes, About,
     Calculators directory — these have high SEO traffic potential with zero
     login required

TIER 2 — HIGH POTENTIAL / MEDIUM EFFORT

3. Pro / Premium Subscription
   - Free tier: public calculators, limited saved estimates (e.g., 5)
   - Pro tier ($9–19/mo): unlimited saves, PDF export, price book, team/crew mode
   - Use Stripe for billing — already have Supabase + auth wired up
   - Contractors who quote daily will pay for reliability and saved history
   - This is the single biggest revenue lever available

4. "Powered by PCC" White-Label for Other Contractors / Small Builders
   - Let local contractors embed your calculators on THEIR website
   - Charge a flat monthly fee ($29–99/mo) for a white-label embed license
   - Low support overhead since your code is already polished
   - This is essentially a B2B SaaS product you already have built

5. PDF Export Upsell (Freemium Gate)
   - Currently behind login — gate PDF export to Pro tier only
   - Free users see a "Upgrade to export" CTA after calculating
   - High conversion moment: they just did the work, now they need the deliverable

6. Supplier / Material Vendor Affiliate Deals
   - Partner with local or national suppliers (lumber yards, concrete suppliers,
     flooring distributors) to place affiliate links in calculator results
   - Example: After a framing estimate, show "Get lumber quotes from XYZ Lumber"
   - Negotiate flat monthly sponsorship ($100–500/mo) or CPA deals
   - More valuable than Amazon because margins are higher in construction supply

TIER 3 — BIGGER SWINGS / LONGER BUILD
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

7. Contractor Lead Generation (Referral Network)
   - Homeowners use your public calculators to get rough estimates
   - Offer them a "Find a Contractor" CTA at the end
   - Charge local contractors a lead fee ($10–50/lead) or flat monthly listing fee
   - Platform: a simple contractor directory with a contact form
   - You already have the traffic from homeowners using calculators

8. Sponsored Field Notes / Blog Posts
   - Your Field Notes section is SEO-rich content territory
   - Charge tool brands, material companies, or software companies for
     sponsored content or "Featured Tool" callouts
   - Disclosure required, but standard in trade publishing
   - Rate: $200–1,000 per sponsored post depending on traffic

9. AI Estimate Assistant (Premium Feature)
   - Add an AI layer that takes a job description in plain English and generates
     a starter estimate across multiple trades
   - "I need to frame a 24x40 garage with a 9-foot ceiling" → auto-fills calculators
   - Gate this as a Pro feature — high perceived value, strong upgrade driver
   - You already have an Anthropic API key in .env

10. Equipment Rental Affiliate Partnerships
    - After large concrete or framing estimates, show a "Rent Equipment" CTA
    - Partner with national rental chains (United Rentals, Sunbelt, Home Depot
      Rentals) — many have affiliate/referral programs
    - Contractors renting equipment = high ticket transactions = better commissions

11. Training / Course Sales
    - Field Notes content + calculator walkthroughs = foundation for a paid course
    - "How to estimate like a pro" short course ($47–97 one-time)
    - Sell via Gumroad, Teachable, or a simple checkout on your own site
    - Low overhead, no recurring cost — pure margin

12. Estimate Template Marketplace
    - Let power users sell their custom estimate templates/setups to other users
    - Take a 20–30% platform cut on each template sale
    - Creates community content without you doing the work
    - Start with your own templates to seed the marketplace
